Arts Stream

Explore Your Future with Arts!

The arts stream offers opportunities in fields like humanities, law, social sciences, fine arts, media, and education.

Here's a categorized list of career-oriented exams for arts students:

1. Humanities and Social Sciences

  • CUET (Common University Entrance Test): For undergraduate programs in central universities across humanities subjects like history, political science, and sociology.
  • TISS BAT (Tata Institute of Social Sciences): For BA programs in social work and allied fields.
  • Ashoka University Entrance: For liberal arts programs at Ashoka University.
  • JNU Entrance Exam: For BA (Hons) in Foreign Languages or other arts subjects (if applicable).

2. Law

  • CLAT (Common Law Admission Test): For 5-year integrated law programs at National Law Universities (NLUs).
  • AILET: For admission to National Law University, Delhi.
  • LSAT India: For private law colleges in India offering integrated law programs.

3. Journalism and Mass Communication

  • IPU CET: For journalism and mass communication courses at Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University.
  • SET (Symbiosis Entrance Test): For journalism and media programs at Symbiosis International University.
  • AJK MCRC Entrance (Jamia Millia Islamia): For media and mass communication programs.

4. Fine Arts and Design

  • NID DAT (National Institute of Design): For design and creative arts programs.
  • NIFT (National Institute of Fashion Technology): For fashion design and management programs.
  • UCEED: For design programs in IITs and other top institutes.

5. Civil Services and Government Exams

  • UPSC Civil Services Exam: After graduation, for roles like IAS, IPS, and IFS. Arts background helps in subjects like history, political science, and sociology.
  • SSC CHSL: For government clerical roles (post-12th eligibility).
  • State PCS: Public service exams conducted by state governments.

6. International Studies

  • SAT: For studying humanities, social sciences, and arts abroad.
  • IELTS/TOEFL: English proficiency exams for studying abroad.
  • GRE: For postgraduate programs in arts, humanities, and social sciences abroad.
