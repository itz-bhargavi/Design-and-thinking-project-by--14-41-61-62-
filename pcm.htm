Mathematics Stream

As a maths student

There are various entrance exams you can take, depending on your career aspirations. Here's a categorized list of important exams in India and internationally:

Engineering & Technology

  • Main & JEE Advanced: For admission to IITs, NITs, and other top engineering colleges.
  • State-Level Exams: (e.g., MHT-CET, WBJEE, AP EAMCET, KCET).
    • Private College Exams:
    • VITEEE (VIT University)
    • SRMJEEE (SRM University)
    • BITSAT (Birla Institute of Technology and Science).
  • International:SAT (for engineering programs abroad).

Economics, Finance, and Business

  • DUET (Delhi University Entrance Test for Economics and related courses).
  • CUET for central universities offering Economics/Mathematics.
  • CPT (Common Proficiency Test): Entry to Chartered Accountancy.
  • GRE (for master's programs in economics/finance abroad).

Architecture

  • NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture).
  • JEE Main Paper 2 (B.Arch/B.Planning).

Defence

  • NDA (National Defence Academy): Maths is critical for the written exam.
  • TES (Technical Entry Scheme): For engineering-related roles in the defence forces.

Mathematics & Statistics

  • ISI Admission Test: Indian Statistical Institute (for B.Stat, B.Math programs).
  • CUET (UG): Central universities in India.
  • IISER Aptitude Test: For integrated BS-MS courses in IISERs.
