Commerce Stream

Explore Your Future with Commerce!

The commerce stream offers a wide range of opportunities in business, finance, accounting, management, and law.

Here's a categorized list of career-oriented exams for commerce students:

1. Accounting and Finance

  • CA (Chartered Accountancy): Administered by ICAI, the CA pathway starts with the CA Foundation exam, followed by Intermediate and Final levels.
  • CMA (Cost and Management Accounting): Conducted by ICMAI, for careers in cost management and financial control.
  • CS (Company Secretary): Conducted by ICSI, for legal and corporate governance roles.

2. Management and Business Administration

  • DU JAT (Delhi University Joint Admission Test): For admission to management and business-related courses in DU colleges.
  • IPMAT (Integrated Program in Management Aptitude Test): For integrated management programs at IIMs (Indore, Rohtak, etc.).
  • NPAT: For BBA and management courses at NMIMS University.
  • SET (Symbiosis Entrance Test): For BBA and related courses at Symbiosis International University.

3. Economics and Statistics

  • CUET (Common University Entrance Test): For undergraduate economics and commerce programs at central universities.
  • ISI Admission Test: For admission to B.Stat (Bachelor of Statistics) or B.Math programs at Indian Statistical Institute.
  • DUET: For economics honors programs at Delhi University (if applicable).

4. Law

  • CLAT (Common Law Admission Test): For admission to 5-year integrated law programs at NLUs.
  • AILET: For admission to National Law University, Delhi.
  • LSAT India: For admission to private law colleges in India.

5. Banking and Government Exams

  • SBI Clerk/PO: For banking careers after graduation (preparation can begin early).
  • RRB (Regional Rural Banks): Banking roles in rural financial institutions.
  • SSC CHSL: For clerical roles in government organizations (post-12th eligibility).

6. International Exams

  • GMAT: For admission to global business and management programs.
  • SAT: For undergraduate studies in commerce-related fields abroad.
  • IELTS/TOEFL: For English proficiency, mandatory for studying abroad.
