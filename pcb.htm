Biology Stream

Explore Your Future with Biology!

A biology background offers diverse career opportunities across healthcare, research, environmental sciences, biotechnology, and more.

Here's a categorized list of career options for biology students:

1. Medical Entrance Exams (MBBS/BDS)

  • NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test): Mandatory for admission to MBBS, BDS, and other undergraduate medical courses in India.
  • AIIMS MBBS: Admission to All India Institute of Medical Sciences (integrated into NEET).
  • JIPMER: Admission to Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research (integrated into NEET).

2. Allied Health Sciences

  • NEET: For courses like B.Sc Nursing, BPT, and other paramedical programs in many colleges.
  • State-Level Exams: Specific state medical entrance tests for allied health sciences.
  • Private Institution Exams: E.g., Manipal Entrance Test (MET), CMC Vellore Entrance Test.

3. Biotechnology and Research

  • IISER Aptitude Test: For admission to integrated BS-MS programs in IISERs.
  • CUET: For undergraduate courses in central universities.
  • JEE Biotechnology: Some institutes accept JEE for B.Tech in Biotechnology.

4. Veterinary Sciences

  • NEET: Required for admission to BVSc (Bachelor of Veterinary Science).

5. International Medical Exams

  • MCAT: For admission to medical schools in the USA and Canada.
  • BMAT: For medical schools in the UK and other countries.
  • UCAT: For undergraduate medical and dental programs in the UK.
